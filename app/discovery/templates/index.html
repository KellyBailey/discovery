About Discovery

Discovery

Discovery is a market research tool for GSA Contract Vehicles. Currently, it features GSA IDIQ vehicles, with more contracts coming in the future. Use Discovery to search vendor history for the awarded contractors and NAICs of the various IDIQs. Some contracts are separated into Unrestricted and Small Business (SB) based on their contract structure. For all other contracts, you can apply small business filters to narrow your results based on any set aside needs you have.

Contract Vehicles

One Acquisition Solution for Integrated Services (OASIS)

  • Specifically designed for the acquisition of professional services
  • Enables the use of ANY contract type
  • All vendors have DCAA-vetted financial systems
  • Download the NAICS description document showing NAICS Codes per pool
  • Learn more about OASIS

Building Maintenance and Operations (BMO)

  • Provides a broad and comprehensive range of BMO services for use throughout
  • Provides a regionally focused pool of highly qualified large and small contractors
  • Allows flexibility for contract types, including fixed price, labor hour, time and materials, and hybrids
  • Leverages spend in acquiring services in an effective, economical, and expedient manner resulting in both significant cost and time savings
  • Following FAR Subpart 16.505 Ordering Procedures, all BMO vendors that can meet the exact specifications of your requirement should be notified (given Fair Opportunity to compete)
  • BMO Phase II Service Category Descriptions
  • Learn more about BMO

Human Capital and Training Solutions (HCaTS)

  • Result of partnership between Office of Personnel Management (OPM) and General Services Administration (GSA)
  • Provides customized Training and Development, Human Capital Strategy, and Organizational Performance Improvement Services
  • Highest Technically Rated with Fair and Reasonable (HTR-FRP) Contractors that have been pre-screened by HC specialists
  • Leveraged Category and Performance Management Strength
  • Learn more about HCaTS

Professional Services Schedule (PSS)

  • Allows use of streamlined procurement procedures in FAR Subpart 8.4 Federal Supply Schedules
  • Provides direct access to simple or complex fixed-price or labor-hour professional services
  • Offers federal agencies access to more than 3,300 experienced contractors
  • Please note, PSS small business designation may be incorrect for up to 20% of listings due to lack of updates by vendors
  • Learn more about PSS

Data Sources

SAM

last updated We pull SAM data directly from the System for Award Management (SAM) to provide supplemental information on all of the vendors on the included contracts, including their SAM registration date, any potential suspensions or debarments, and the NAICS codes that a vendor may contract under. Set aside information is based on contract awarded designations and may not be consistent with the current SAM registration. Contract awarded designations are valid for the term of the contract.
Contractors are required to re-certify size/socioeconimic at least annually in SAM in accordance with FAR 4.120(b)(1). Contrast that with the contract frequency every five years (or Novation/merger) in accordance with FAR 52.219-28. Hence the size/socioeconomic status shown in SAM may differ from the contract and thus cannot be relied upon for orders issued under the contract which default to the size/socioeconomic status in the contract. Refer to the contract vehicle website to determine the current size/socioeconomic status of a particular contractor under that contract vehicle. )

FPDS

last updated Discovery loads data from the Federal Procurement Data System (FPDS) to automatically provide you with a context-rich contract history for each vendor, including the amount of the contract, the primary NAICS code and the pricing type.
